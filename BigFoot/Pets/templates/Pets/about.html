We are Petsitters

Are you leaving somewhere and you have no one to leave your beloved pet with, or do you just have no time to go for a walk with your beloved dog?

We guarantee that your pet will be treated like a member of their own family. For peace of mind on the road, simply find the right sitter to take care of your pet for as long as it takes - be it just for a weekend or for a few weeks. Petsitter services are suitable for dogs and cats of all ages, including puppies and kittens, and are the perfect solution for pet owners looking for a safe, comfortable alternative to hotels.

And if you just want to relax or there are other reasons why you cannot walk with your dog, then our walking specialists will be happy to help you and take a walk with your pet.

A pet care service you can trust

All sitters and dog walkers are screened for competence and a safety test. All profiles collected on the site are pre-checked to ensure the required competencies of the account owner.

Nobody knows your pet better than you. Use our advanced search to find the most suitable sitter or walker who will provide proper care your favorite for all its unique needs.

Pet owners and sitters can arrange a personal meeting after booking to discuss all the details and make sure everything meets your expectations.

Мы всегда рядом, когда вы нуждаетесь в нас - 24/7.

If you need help with a booking, veterinarian advice or assistance in a difficult situation, our trust and safety team is always ready to help you and your pets.
